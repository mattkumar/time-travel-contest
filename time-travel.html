About

This table is a recreation of the time travel device seen in the movie Back to the Future. It uses a combination of scss and {reactable} to stylize and display the table. The {countup} package by John Coene is integrated into the body of the table to give the illusion of the machine calibrating.

Note: If you miss the counter animation due to reading this, simply refresh the page
